Elon Musk

Elon Reeve Musk (born June 28, 1971) is an entrepreneur and business magnate. He is the founder, CEO, and Chief Engineer at SpaceX, early-stage investor, CEO, and Product Architect of Tesla.

Musk is one of the richest people in the world. He briefly attended the University of Pretoria before moving to Canada at age 17 to attend Queen's University.
